More about me

I've worn a number of different hats over the years: I've worked on websites, distributed backends, cloud infrastructure along with a healthy dose of scripting and spreadsheet work. I have a Computer Science degree from the University of Waterloo and worked as a backend/architectural engineer for Noom in New York City for 5 years. Prior to that, I worked a number of contract jobs that gave me a large breadth of experience with different teams, technologies and business needs. There's a variety of tools in my toolbelt and over the years, I've learned to take the long view; it's not enough for something to work, it has to work reliably and predictably. I love digging into a problem deeply and building things that make people's lives easier. In my free time, I like to work on exploratory projects based on my own interests, from chess to bikes to crosswords.

In 2024, I got to marry my best friend Lauren in a small ceremony in Mallorca, Spain. We've had the opportunity to travel to and live in many different countries together, with our latest move being to Australia to be closer to Lauren's mom for 2025.
